# 📝 napkin - Create simple drawings on your screen

## ✏️ Getting Started

Launch Napkin by double-clicking the icon on your desktop. You see a white canvas expand across your monitor. Click and drag your mouse to leave a mark. The tool remembers every stroke you make. If you use a tablet, the software detects the pressure of your pen. This creates thin or thick lines based on how hard you press.

* Pen Support: Connect a drawing tablet to use natural handwriting.
* Minimal Interface: The tool stays out of your way. Your focus remains on the work.
* Low Resource Usage: The app runs in the background without slowing your computer.

## 🖱️ Using a Tablet

Napkin integrates with devices like Wacom or other digital pads. Plug the tablet into your USB port before you open the app. The software recognizes the tablet as a primary input device. Move your pen across the pad to draw lines. Use the side buttons on your pen to switch between tools if your tablet software supports custom functions.
